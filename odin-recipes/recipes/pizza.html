Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a classic dish made with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and your favorite toppings. It's versatile, delicious, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Toppings - pepperoni, vegetables, sausage, etc.
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C)
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface
  3. Transfer dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough
  5. Add mozzarella cheese and your desired toppings
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle seasoning
  7. Bake for 10–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly
  8. Remove, slice, and serve hot
