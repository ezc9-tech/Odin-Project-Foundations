Curry Recipe

Decription

Curry is a very seasoned pastey soup like food. It consists of vegetables, protein, curry paste, and rice.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables - Can be whatever vegetables you like
  • Protein - Chicken, Beef, or Pork
  • Curry Paste - mild or spicy

Steps

  1. Stir fry your vegetables and protein for 5 minutes in a skillet
  2. Add water and bring to a boil
  3. Lower heat and simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Turn off heat and throw in curry paste
  5. Stir in curry paste
  6. Lastly, simmer for five more minutes
