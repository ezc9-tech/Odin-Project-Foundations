Marry Me Chicken Pasta Recipe

Description

Marry Me Chicken Pasta is a creamy, flavorful dish made with tender chicken, garlic, sun-dried tomatoes, and a rich parmesan cream sauce tossed with pasta. It's so delicious, it might just inspire a proposal!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast - sliced or cubed
  • Pasta - penne or fettuccine works well
  • Garlic - minced
  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Chicken broth
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions and set aside
  2. Season and cook chicken in a skillet until golden and cooked through, then remove
  3. In the same skillet, sauté garlic and sun-dried tomatoes
  4. Add chicken broth and heavy cream, then bring to a simmer
  5. Stir in parmesan cheese and seasoning until sauce thickens
  6. Return chicken to the skillet and combine
  7. Add cooked pasta and toss until fully coated in sauce
  8. Serve warm and enjoy
